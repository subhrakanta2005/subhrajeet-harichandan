The Author

Born to Write,
Built by Life

Subhrajeet Harichandan was born on 6 September 2005 in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. A voracious reader from childhood, he completed his schooling at MBS Public School and pursued graduation in Commerce at Buxi Jagabandhu Autonomous College.

His debut novel Love Will Find You was released by Notion Press in 2022 — while still in secondary school — making him one of India's youngest published novelists. Writing has always been his refuge from heartbreak, growth, and the complexities of young life.

Beyond writing, he is passionate about cricket, chess, and singing.
